Getting Started: The Homepage

Open your web browser and go to the EZR Workflow website.

Sign In or Sign Up:

Existing Users: Click on the 'Sign In' button and enter your credentials.

New Users: Click on the 'Get Started' or 'Sign Up' button to create a new account.

Explore:

Take a moment to explore the homepage and get familiar with the introductory content and features highlighted.

Navigating Your Dashboard

Access the Dashboard:

Once logged in, you'll be directed to your Dashboard. This is your control center for managing tasks and schedules.

Task Summary:

Get a quick overview of your upcoming tasks in the 'Snapshot' section.

Mini-Calendar:

Use the mini-calendar to see your tasks for the week.

Quick Links:

Utilize quick links to create a new task, view all tasks, or set reminders.

Team Updates:

If you're part of a team, check this section for collaborative tasks or team updates.
